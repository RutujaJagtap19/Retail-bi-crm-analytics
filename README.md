# Business Intelligence & CRM Analytics for Retail Expansion

## Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end Business Intelligence (BI) and CRM analytics solution designed for a multi-country electronics retail business.

The solution focuses on sales performance analysis, customer segmentation, and data-driven decision-making to support market expansion into Ireland.

---

## Business Problem
The business lacked centralized visibility of sales and customer data, customer segmentation, and insights to support online sales growth and regional expansion.

---

## Solution
- Designed a star-schema data model with fact and dimension tables
- Built interactive Power BI dashboards using DAX
- Performed customer segmentation based on customer lifetime value and spending patterns
- Integrated Salesforce CRM to simulate marketing campaigns and customer case management

---

## Dashboards
- Sales Performance Overview
- Category Performance Analysis
- Customer Insights Dashboard
- CRM Customer Segmentation Dashboard

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (DAX, Power Query)
- Salesforce CRM
- Data Modeling (Star Schema)
- Business Intelligence & Analytics

---

## Key Insights
- Identified top-performing products and categories
- Analyzed online vs offline sales trends
- Highlighted regional performance with focus on Ireland
- Enabled personalized marketing strategies using customer segmentation
